Peanut Butter & Jelly

the worlds greatest sandwich

The peanut butter and jelly sandwhich is a staple in American households. A sandwich consisting of two peices of sliced bread, peanut butter and jelly first appeared in 1901 in the Boston Cooking School Magazine of Culinary Science and Domestic Economics written by Julia Davis Chandler. She said to use currant or crab-apple jelly and called the combination delicious and as far as she knew, original.
